Aircraft Conceptual Design & Wing Structural Analysis

Designed and built a remote-controlled aircraft, optimizing performance within defined constraints to maximize range, stability, maneuverability, and payload capacity.

Project Overview

Designed and built a remote-controlled (RC) aircraft over the course of a semester, optimizing performance within defined constraints to maximize range, stability, maneuverability, and payload capacity. The project required translating theoretical aerodynamic concepts into a functional, flight-ready aircraft.

The Problem

The objective was to develop an aircraft that could:

  • Meet strict dimensional and payload constraints
  • Successfully take off, maintain stable and controllable flight, and land
  • Travel a specified distance efficiently
  • Balance weight, lift, and structural integrity

My Contributions

  • Took on a project coordination role, helping organize tasks, track progress, and align team members across design and build phases
  • Contributed to conceptual aircraft configuration, wing sizing, and performance trade-off analysis
  • Led the finite element analysis (FEA) of the wing structure to evaluate stress distribution and predicted deflection under aerodynamic loading
  • Supported fabrication and assembly of the wing and fuselage components
  • Assisted in troubleshooting structural alignment and integration challenges during build

Final Outcome / Impact

  • Delivered a fully functional, flight-ready RC aircraft
  • Successfully completed takeoff, sustained flight, and landing
  • Demonstrated integration of conceptual design, structural validation, and hands-on fabrication
  • Applied analytical tools and team coordination to execute the full engineering design cycle under time constraints

Key Skills Applied

  • Aerodynamic design & performance analysis
  • Finite element analysis (FEA) & structural validation
  • Constraint-based engineering design
  • CAD modeling & technical documentation
  • Hands-on fabrication & assembly
  • Project coordination & team collaboration
  • Engineering trade-off analysis
