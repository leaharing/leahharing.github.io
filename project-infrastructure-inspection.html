Infrastructure Inspection Project

Planned and executed tower inspections across Newfoundland, coordinating contractors and internal teams, access requirements, tower shutdowns, and logistics.

Project Overview

Participated in a five-day remote infrastructure inspection trip across Northern Newfoundland, visiting approximately 10 coastal sites including Norris Point, St. Anthony, L'Anse aux Meadows, and more. The trip involved coordinating tower inspections, overseeing contractors, managing site logistics, and ensuring alignment between engineering teams and field personnel.

Across two work terms, I supported multiple additional site visits, including remote helicopter-access locations and interprovincial travel for meetings and inspections.

The Problem

Coastal infrastructure requires regular inspection and maintenance, often in logistically complex and weather-sensitive environments.

The challenge was coordinating contractor activities, managing inspection schedules and shutdown periods, and ensuring work was completed safely, on time, and within scope.

My Contributions

  • Coordinated inspection schedules and managed site logistics, including temporary shutdown periods
  • Oversaw contractor work at multiple sites to ensure alignment with project requirements
  • Managed project documentation and inspection records
  • Supported budgeting and scope tracking
  • Communicated updates between contractors, engineers, and operations personnel
  • Maintained organization across multiple active sites to align with the five-day schedule

Final Outcome / Impact

  • Successfully completed multi-site inspections across remote locations within schedule
  • Ensured contractor alignment and adherence to scope
  • Supported safe, organized execution in logistically challenging environments
  • Strengthened communication between field teams and engineering stakeholders

Key Skills Applied

  • Project coordination
  • Contractor oversight
  • Scheduling & logistics management
  • Risk awareness in remote operations
  • Stakeholder communication
  • Documentation & reporting
  • Adaptability in dynamic environments
