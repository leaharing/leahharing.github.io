About Me

I’m a Mechanical Engineer (EIT) registered with the Professional Engineers and Geoscientists Newfoundland and Labrador (PEGNL), with experience working in the energy industry, marine safety, and biomedical research. My experience has been in environments where timelines are tight, and I’m used to staying organized and keeping things moving.

I’ve supported projects that involved coordinating across teams, managing documentation, tracking progress, and helping move work from planning through execution. Working in regulated environments taught me how to balance competing priorities, manage risk, and keep stakeholders aligned, especially when timelines are tight or requirements change.

I enjoy working with people and solving complex problems in a way that makes sense in the real world.

Outside of work, I spend time reading, coaching hockey, and staying active. Whether it’s sports, being outdoors, or baking for friends and family, I enjoy filling my time with things that keep me moving and connected to the people around me.
